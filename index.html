About BIGULE

BIGULE is an offline-first family safety application developed by Vandorix Solutions. Instead of depending on cloud servers or internet access, BIGULE uses SMS to deliver SOS alerts, heartbeat updates and critical safety notifications. Designed for families, travellers and rural communities, it continues to protect when mobile data is unavailable.

Why BIGULE?
